RockPaperScissors

Rock paper scissors is an intransitive hand game, usually played between two people, in which each player simultaneously forms one of three options: rock, paper, or scissors.

TicTacToe

A game in which two players seek in alternate turns to complete a row, a column, or a diagonal with either three O's or three X's drawn in the spaces of a grid of nine squares.

Connect4

A game in which the players choose a color and then take turns dropping colored tokens into a six-row, seven-column vertically suspended grid trying to get 4 in a row.
